The Crows Conformity

As an artist, I feel the need to experiment with different techniques and to constantly improve on my skills while also touching on interesting concepts, for example, playing with relationships between technology, art, and culture.

My concept, 'The Crow's Conformity,' is about capturing the essence of consumerism in a way that's both interesting and new. I have made a 3D-printed sculpture of a crow entangled in glowing RGB wires leaning over a stimulating screen with constant scrolling of social media images.

This artwork is about creating a thoughtful experience that invites the viewers to reflect on their own everyday habits. I invite the viewer to self-reflect on who is really in control. I hope to create an art piece that challenges people's perceptions of contemporary consumer culture.
