About Me

As an artist driven by curiosity and creativity, I've always been fascinated by the intersection of traditional techniques and digital tools. My journey into 3D modelling began in 2021, marking a new chapter in my artistic life.

I am a designer for a sculpture company and an upcoming graduate with a Bachelor of Visual Arts degree. I specialize in creating realistic character-driven designs using Blender, which is essential to my work, enabling me to bring creative visions to life.

As the founder of 3D-OZWORX, my goal is to create captivating, high-quality art that inspires others. I'm committed to pushing boundaries within 3D art, exploring new technologies, and showcasing designs globally.
